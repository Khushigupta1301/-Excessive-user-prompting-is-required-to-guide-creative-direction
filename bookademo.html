COMMON QUESTIONS

How long is a typical demo? +
Our standard demo runs 30 minutes. For enterprise accounts with complex requirements, we can extend to 60 minutes to cover integrations, security, and custom workflows in depth.
Who should attend the demo? +
Ideally the decision-maker and at least one technical stakeholder. We'll tailor the session to cover both product value and technical implementation so everyone walks away with clarity.
Is there a minimum contract for Enterprise? +
Enterprise contracts start at 10 seats with annual billing. We offer flexible terms including monthly pilots for teams that want to evaluate before committing. Our sales team will find what works best for you.
Can I see a live demo before booking? +
Yes! Check out our interactive product tour at eup.io/tour — it walks through the core builder, prompt library, and analytics dashboard in about 5 minutes with no sign-up required.
Do you support on-premise deployment? +
Yes, for enterprise customers we offer private cloud and on-premise deployment options with full SOC 2 Type II compliance, SSO (SAML/OIDC), and custom data residency agreements.
How quickly can my team get onboarded? +
Most teams are fully onboarded within 1 week. We provide a dedicated onboarding specialist, documentation, and async Slack support during your first 30 days — included in all paid plans.
