PRIVACY POLICY

Effective date: March 7, 2026 · Last updated: March 7, 2026

We only collect data needed to provide EUP services, secure your account, and improve product performance.

WHAT WE COLLECT

We collect account details, workspace usage events, and support messages. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

  • Account info: name, email, profile metadata
  • Service data: prompt metadata, run statistics, logs
  • Billing data: plan and payment status via payment providers

HOW WE USE DATA

  • Provide account access, workspace sync, and analytics
  • Protect against abuse, fraud, and unauthorized access
  • Send transactional emails like verification and password reset
  • Improve reliability, performance, and product features

RETENTION & SECURITY

Data is retained as long as required to provide service and comply with law. We use encryption at rest/in transit and role-based access controls for internal systems.

YOUR RIGHTS

You can request access, correction, or deletion of personal data by contacting support. You can also close your account at any time.
